Goals of Flood Risk Management

Identify and assess areas at risk of flooding to understand the potential impact and prioritize mitigation efforts.Implement measures to prevent or reduce the occurrence of floods, such as building flood barriers and improving drainage systems.Develop and implement emergency response plans to effectively manage flood events and minimize damage to lives and property.Engage with communities to raise awareness about flood risks, promote preparedness, and foster resilience.
